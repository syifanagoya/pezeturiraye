Introduction :

  • Presenting mainly the sports news at KTTV Fox 11 network, Liz Habib is an award-winning anchor and journalist, with years of experience.
  • Having reported extensively on the return of the NFL to Los Angeles, Liz has reported extensively on the progress of the Los Angeles Rams.
  • Making regular appearances on LA, Studio 11 LA and the 10 pm news on Fox 11, she has also contributed to Fox Sports 1, Fox News and CNN.
  • She joined KTTV in the year 2005 and covered the Lakers NBA Championship wins in 2009 and 2010 & the Stanley Cup victories of the Anaheim Ducks in the year 2007. However, she became the main sports anchor only in the year 2011.
  • Post becoming the main anchor, Habib covered and reported the Stanley Cup victories of the Los Angeles Kings in 2012 and 2014.

Personal Life : Parents, Family and Education

  • Liz is married to Charles Whittaker.
  • Her father was a collegiate swimmer and sports runs in the family as she has 6 brothers, out of which 4 were associated with Division 1 sports in college.
  • Due to their work commitments Liz and her husband have to frequently shuttle between Orange County and Los Angeles. They have 2 pet dogs which are named Wiley and Teddy.
  • Liz Habib was part of the Class of 1988 batch of English and Journalism students at The University of Pittsburgh.
  • She was also a student at St. Francis Academy in Pittsburgh.

TV Career, Job, Salary and Net Worth :

  • Liz has worked as a news anchor at KTVK in Phoenix & as an anchor/ reporter at WKYC in Cleveland. Previously, she was also associated with WTOV-TV in Steubenville/ Wheeling as an anchor/ reporter.
  • Liz has been the main sports anchor at KTTV Fox, Los Angeles since 2005 and presents the Fox 11 overtime NFL and college post-game shows and Fox 11 Extra Innings which is aired right after the MLB Games.
  • As part of her work assignment, Liz also reports & covers the Los Angeles Dodgers and Los Angeles Angels MLB teams.
  • During this time, she also focused on NBA teams Los Angeles Lakers & Los Angeles Clippers. The other teams that she covers are the Los Angeles Kings & Anaheim Ducks of the NHL, and Los Angeles Galaxy of MLS.
  • Habib also frequently covers local college games. The college teams that she reports on are mainly, USC and UCLA.
