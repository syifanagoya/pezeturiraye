G.E.M.’s Music Career and Album Sales

G.E.M. started her music career in 2008. She quickly became famous with her powerful voice and stage presence. Her debut studio album, G.E.M., reached number one in Hong Kong. Her music revenue extends beyond album sales. Streaming platforms like YouTube play a crucial role. G.E.M. is the first female Chinese singer with five music videos exceeding 100 million views. These streams generate substantial income, boosting her financial profile.

Income from Concerts and Tours

Concerts and tours significantly increase G.E.M.’s income. Her X.X.X. Live Tour and Queen of Hearts Tour were major successes.

FAQs about G.E.M.

Who is G.E.M.?

G.E.M., born Gloria Tang Tsz-kei on August 16, 1991, is a Chinese singer-songwriter known for her powerful vocals and dynamic stage presence. She made her debut in 2008 and quickly rose to fame in the Hong Kong music industry.

What does G.E.M. stand for?

She stands for Get Everybody Moving. It’s a backronym chosen to reflect her energetic and motivational musical style.

What are some of G.E.M.’s biggest hits?

Some of G.E.M.’s biggest hits include What Have U Done, Bubble, and Goodbye. Her music often tops charts in Hong Kong and mainland China.

What is G.E.M.’s musical background?

She grew up in a musical family. Her mother graduated from the Shanghai Conservatory of Music, her uncle was a violinist, her grandfather was a saxophonist, and her grandmother taught her to sing. She started writing songs and participating in competitions at a young age.

When did she gain widespread popularity?

She gained widespread popularity after appearing on the Chinese singing competition I Am a Singer in 2014, where she finished in second place. This appearance significantly boosted her fame in mainland China.

How many albums has she released?

As of now, she has released over five albums since her debut. These include G.E.M., 18…, MySecret, Xposed, and Heartbeat.

What awards has she won?

She has won numerous awards, including Best Female Vocalist Mandarin at the Golden Melody Awards, and she has been nominated for the World Music Awards and the MTV Europe Music Awards.

What is G.E.M.’s vocal range?

She is known for her wide vocal range, earning her nicknames like Girl with Giant Lungs and Young Diva with Giant Lungs.

Has she acted in any films?

Yes, she made her film debut in 2009 in Trick or Cheat and voiced a lead role in the 2017 animated film Charming alongside Demi Lovato and Avril Lavigne.
