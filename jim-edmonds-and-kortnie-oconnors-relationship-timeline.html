Jim Edmonds and Kortnie O'Connor's Relationship Timeline

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, April 22, 2024

Next step: “I do!” Jim Edmonds hasn’t always been lucky in love, but after sparking a romance with Kortnie O’Connor, things started looking up.

The former MLB center fielder tied the knot three times before going public with O’Connor in April 2020. He shares two children with his first wife, Lee Ann Horton, who passed away in 2015. He also welcomed a son and daughter with second wife Allison Jayne Raski, from whom he split in 2014.

In October 2014, Edmonds exchanged vows with Meghan King, who joined the cast of The Real Housewives of Orange County shortly after the wedding. The pair welcomed daughter Aspen in November 2016 and twin sons Hayes and Hart in June 2018. Two years after the boys’ arrival, the duo called it quits, with King claiming that Edmonds had been unfaithful with a nanny. (He denied the allegations at the time.)

As the dramatic breakup continued, the Bravo star opened up about having a threesome with Edmonds and another woman in the early days of their marriage. “I knew that Jim had this bad boy kind of past,” she said during a January 2020 episode of her “Intimate Knowledge” podcast, noting that she chose someone she was “comfortable” to help spice up her sex life with Edmonds.

The same woman, King alleged, had joined the California native on a Mexican getaway for his birthday. “This threesome girl,” she said. “This girl who I thought was my friend. … I think they’ve probably had sex more than the one time.”

Us Weekly later confirmed that the friend in question was O’Connor, who quarantined with Edmonds amid the coronavirus pandemic in early 2020. The couple made their romance Instagram official that April.

Following a slew of public spats, King and Edmonds finalized their divorce in May 2021. “I feel the same as I did before it was finalized,” the Missouri native told Us exclusively. “I think it’s, like, an energetic closure, which is important, but I mean, I’ve been learning how to be independent ever since we separated. … It feels good.”

At the time, King noted that she was “having a lot of fun dating,” but craved a strong partnership like the one Edmonds found with O’Connor, who he proposed to in July 2021.
