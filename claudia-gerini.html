Claudia Gerini

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, January 27, 2024

by andrew · February 19, 2017

photo: prphotos.com

Place of Birth: Rome, Lazio, Italy

Date of Birth: 18 December, 1971

Ethnicity: Italian

Claudia Gerini is an Italian actress and showgirl. She had a role in the American film John Wick: Chapter 2 (2017). She won the National Competition of Miss Teenager in 1985.

Claudia is the daughter of Tania and Antonio Gerini. She has a sister, Romina.

Claudia’s maternal grandfather was named Giuseppe Cerere. Giuseppe was from Afragola, Campania.
