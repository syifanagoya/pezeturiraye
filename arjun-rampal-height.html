Arjun Rampal Wiki and Bio

Arjun Rampal Wikipedia: - Rampal was born on 26 November 1972 in Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh, India. He is an Indian actor, model, film producer and television personality who works in Hindi films. He made his acting debut in Rajiv Rai's love story Pyaar Ishq Aur Mohabbat and has since appeared in more than 40 movies. He has won numerous accolades for his work in Rock On!!, including the National Film Award for Best Supporting Actor and the Filmfare Award for Best Supporting Actor. With I See You, under his production company Chasing Ganesha Films, Rampal made his filmmaking debut. He also produced the biopic Daddy, for which he co-wrote the screenplay.

Arjun Rampal Career

Arjun Rampal Career: - Rampal was scheduled to make his Telugu film debut in the part of Aurangzeb, the Mughal Emperor, in Hari Hara Veera Mallu, however, he withdrew due to other obligations. He has played the lead roles in a number of romance movies, such as Deewaanapan, Dil Hai Tumhaara, Dil Ka Rishta, Vaada, and I See You, as well as action movies Asambhav and D-Day, thrillers Yakeen and Roy, sociopolitical dramas Chakravyuh and Roy, and biopics Daddy.  As one of the three judges for the fourth season of the dance reality competition Nach Baliye on STAR Plus in 2008, Rampal made his television debut.  Aankhen, which became his first commercially successful role, marked a turning point for Rampal in the year 2002. Don (2006), Om Shanti Om (2007), Housefull (2010), Raajneeti (2010), Ra. One (2011), Kahaani 2: Durga Rani Singh (2016), and Nail Polish (2021) are some of his other financially successful movies. Rampal made his online film debut in 2021 with Nail Polish. He played the lead in Dhaakad in 2022. Rampal featured in Rock On!! 2 in 2016, the follow-up to Rock On!
