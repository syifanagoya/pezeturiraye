What is the code in Hello Neighbor 2?

In order to find this code, you will require the ultraviolet lamp that can be found in either the bathroom or bedroom. Then you’ll need to find the code itself, which is written in invisible ink behind a crookedly hung painting. When the player discovers the code, it will be seen in this sequence: 4785.

Where is the crowbar in Act 2?

It is located in Mr.Peterson’s House on the second floor behind a large grate. To get it, first you need to get a fire extinguisher.

What is the Blue Key for in Hello Neighbor Act 2?

The blue key allows the player access to the room on the 3rd floor containing the crowbar which is gated and you need to go behind the big apple painting. There is also the platform that takes you to Fear Supermarket which you need to throw an object at the lever in order to access.

What does 2369 mean in Hello Neighbor?

The red gramophone whithers the plants so you can open the door but the only thing there is a mannequin in a room with a chair and a box or two. The 2369 button code activates the moving platform but there is nothing on the other end than mannequin and an empty closet.

How do you get your phone in Hello Neighbor 2?

Where To Find The Secret Phone in Hello Neighbor 2. The Secret Phone can be found on the pathway up towards the mountains. The player will need to do some platforming to reach it. A small platform being held up by 4 wooden legs will feature an orange ground attached to a blue wall.

How do you get the shovel in Hello Neighbor Act 2?

In the final game, the shovel can be found in the tool room in Act 1, though it seemingly has no function. In Act 2, the shovel is instead in the flooded room guarded by a Sharkotron, and the player must drain the water in order to stop the Sharkotron from capturing them and causing them to be put back in the basement.

How do you get the car keys in Hello Neighbor?

It can be found on the second floor. You first have to access the room next to the red key room by knocking down the painting and jumping through the hole in the wall. The car key will then be located on the left.

How do you get into the shed in Hello Neighbor?

After grabbing the pick, go inside the open window on the side of the house (the one with the black wire running inside) and open the door to your left. In the hallway, you can now use the lock pick to open the door to the left of the staircase. That door leads to the shed where you stole the lock pick.

What do you do with the egg in Hello Neighbor hide and seek?

Take the egg in the nest first. This will summon a large bird and release a small toy. Stand on top near the nest and shoot down a small bird flying on the map. It’s best if you shoot in one place and the bird will fly up and fall into your missiles.

What do I do in the basement of Hello Neighbor?

The player must turn on a generator and then make their way back to the exit door, flipping the switch to remove the gate blocking the door. Upon entering, the Neighbor enters behind them and begins chasing them. The player must make their way to the end of this passageway without being caught.

How do you grow in Hello Neighbor?

Growing. To grow the golden apple tree, you must plant the seed in the plot of soil in the southeast corner of the Neighbor’s yard. You must then get the watering can and water the seed. It will then begin to grow a sapling and, after three hours, the tree will be fully grown.

Who is Mr Peterson in Hello Neighbor?
