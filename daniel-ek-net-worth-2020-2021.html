Early Life & Biography

Daniel Ek was born on 21 February 1983 in Stockholm, Sweden. He spent most of his childhood days in the Rågsved district of Stockholm, Sweden. As a child, he was very much interested in computers and programming. When Ek was in high school, he created several small games and applications.

He completed his high school from IT-Gymnasiet in Sundbyberg in 2002. Later, he attended the KTH Royal Institute of Technology, where he studied engineering. However, he did not complete his graduation and dropped out of college to focus on his IT career.

Personal Life

Daniel Ek married his longtime girlfriend, Sofia Levander, at Lake Como. His marriage was officiated by actor and comedian Chris Rock and singer Bruno Mars performed at his wedding. Several renowned guests also appeared, including Facebook founder Mark Zuckerberg. Together they are the parents of two children.

Age, Height, and Weight

Being born on 21 February 1983, Daniel Ek is 41 years old as of today’s date 20th July 2024. His height is 1.77 m tall, and his weight is 80 kg.

Career

Daniel Ek’s foray into the business world started at the age of thirteen when he used to make business websites for clients from his house. The first amount which he earned from his freelancing business was $100. Later, he started charging $200 from his clients and finally reached upto $5000 per website.

In order to expand his business, he hired students from his school to work with him on the websites. By the age of eighteen, his earning reached upto $50,000 per month and was managing a team of more than twenty-five students. His parents started to notice his work once he began buying expensive electronic gadgets.

Ek worked in a senior position at Nordic auction company Tradera which was later bought by eBay in 2006. He also served as the CTO of Stardoll, a browsing base game community. Ek also started an online advertising company named Advertigo, which he sold to TradeDoubler in 2006. He was also the CEO of μTorrent before it was sold to BitTorrent on December 7 of 2006.

He made enough wealth from selling Advertigo and through his previous work that he thought of retiring. However, Ek realized that money was meaningless if he does not work on something which he is passionate about. The idea of Spotify came in his mind in 2002 when the peer-to-peer music service Napster shut down.

Along with Martin Lorentzon, he launched Spotify in 2006. Two years later, the company launched its legal music streaming service. The service initially ran on a peer-to-peer distribution model, but later switched to a server-client model. In 2015, the co-founder and chairman of Spotify Martin Lorentzon stepped down from his position.

Awards & Achievements

Daniel Ek has been awarded the KTH Great Prize 2012 for his innovative application and successful commercialization of information technology at the highest international level. His company Spotify has 217 million active users and has raised more than $2.5 billion in venture funding.
