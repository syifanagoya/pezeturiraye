EAGLES: Who Is Jake Elliotts Wife Annie?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, March 14, 2024

Annie Elliot, formerly Annie Timyan, is the beautiful wife of American football placekicker, Jake Elliott.

Annie and Jake dated for about three years and spent many fabulous moments together before they tied the knots on March 8, 2020.

They exchanged their vows at Fairmount Park Horticultural Center in what was tagged ‘the crazy cactus wedding’.

“I couldn’t have picked a better man to spend the rest of my life with. This day was more amazing than I could have ever imagined. Thank you to everyone who made my crazy cactus wedding come to life!”, Annie wrote on Instagram.

Annie clearly indicated that she had been crushing on Jake since the good old days and even threw in some quality throwback pictures to show just how far the two go back.

Annie enrolled in Mcclure Jr. High School in Illinois and went further to earn a post-graduate from Auburn University. She worked as a Graduate Assistant at the school.
